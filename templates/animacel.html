Animacel

Animacel biotechnology, a spin-off from the Veterinary Faculty in Ljubljana, was founded in 2011, pioneering a revolutionary technology rooted in advanced stem cell therapy science. Committed to excellence, Animacel has collaborated with partner veterinary clinics to systematically collect detailed data about the indications and therapy outcomes. As part of the Ventures Thrive program, Animacel aimed to deepen their data insights.
Working closely with Animacel’s CEO Luka Mohorič and leveraging data mining techniques, we evaluated the quality of their existing data, gained valuable insights, developed success metrics for the therapy, and examined correlations between dog characteristics and therapy outcomes. The resulting data and analysis are currently being utilized to build strong supporting scientific evidence for developing safe and efficient therapy approaches.
Through this process, we identified challenges in the existing data collection workflow. To address these, we developed a data collection strategy that will free the CEO from the burden of coordinating data exchange with partner clinics. This new approach also fosters greater clinic engagement by offering benefits, such as treatment discounts and insights, encouraging more consistent data sharing.
